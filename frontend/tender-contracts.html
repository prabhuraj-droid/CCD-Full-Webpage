About Tender & Contracts Section

The Tender and Contracts section of Corporate Communications Department handles all tender processes, contract management, and vendor relationships related to communication services, advertising, media buying, event management, and promotional activities.

We ensure transparent, competitive bidding processes in accordance with government norms and NLC's procurement policies, maintaining the highest standards of integrity and efficiency.

📋 Tender Management

Processing tenders for communication services, advertising agencies, event organizers, and media houses.

📑 Contract Administration

Managing contracts, ensuring compliance, monitoring deliverables, and handling vendor payments.

🤝 Vendor Relations

Maintaining professional relationships with approved vendors, agencies, and service providers.

Tender Process

Step 1: Tender Notification

Tenders are published on NLC India's official website, e-procurement portal, and relevant newspapers as per tender value.

Step 2: Document Download

Interested vendors can download tender documents from the e-procurement portal or collect physically from the office.

Step 3: Pre-bid Meeting (if applicable)

Clarifications addressed in pre-bid meetings for complex tenders.

Step 4: Bid Submission

Technical and financial bids submitted online or offline as specified in tender conditions.

Step 5: Evaluation

Technical evaluation followed by financial bid opening for qualifying bidders.

Step 6: Award of Contract

Contract awarded to L1 bidder or based on quality-cum-cost criteria as specified.

⚖️ Transparency

All tenders processed as per government guidelines ensuring fair competition and transparency.

📊 E-Procurement

Online tender submission and evaluation through certified e-procurement platforms.

✅ Compliance

Strict adherence to CVC guidelines, GFR rules, and NLC's procurement policies.

Vendor Eligibility Criteria

General eligibility requirements for participating in CCD tenders:

  • Valid GST registration
  • PAN card and income tax returns
  • Relevant experience in similar work (minimum 3-5 years)
  • Financial capacity as per tender requirements
  • Valid licenses/certifications for specialized services
  • No blacklisting by government/PSU organizations
  • Compliance with labor laws and statutory requirements
  • EMD/bid security as specified in tender

Contract Management

Post-award contract administration includes:

  • Execution of formal agreement
  • Performance bank guarantee submission
  • Regular monitoring of deliverables
  • Quality assessment and compliance checks
  • Invoice verification and payment processing
  • Contract renewal/extension as applicable
  • Performance evaluation reports
  • Dispute resolution mechanism

Important Information for Bidders

  • Check tender notifications regularly on NLC website
  • Download complete tender documents carefully
  • Attend pre-bid meetings for clarifications
  • Submit all required documents as per checklist
  • Ensure bid validity period is maintained
  • Follow instructions regarding EMD payment
  • Submit technical and financial bids separately as required
  • No physical bids accepted after online submission deadline
  • Corrigendum/amendments published on website only
  • NLC reserves the right to accept/reject any bid

Vendor Registration

For empanelment with NLC Corporate Communications:

  • Submit company profile and credentials
  • Provide portfolio of past work
  • Include client references
  • Attach financial documents
  • Specify service categories of interest
  • Registration valid for specified period
  • Periodic renewal required

Payment Terms

Standard payment terms for CCD contracts:

  • Payment against satisfactory completion of work
  • Invoice submission with required documents
  • GST applicable as per prevailing rates
  • TDS deduction as per Income Tax Act
  • Payment within 30-45 days of invoice approval
  • Penalty clause for delayed/deficient work
  • Bank account details verification mandatory
