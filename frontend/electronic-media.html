Electronic Media

Managing broadcast media relations and digital content distribution

About Electronic Media Section

The Electronic Media section manages NLC India Limited's presence across television, radio, and digital broadcasting platforms. We coordinate with news channels, produce video content, and ensure effective communication through electronic channels.

Our team maintains relationships with electronic media houses, handles interview requests, arranges press conferences, and monitors media coverage to maintain NLC's positive image in the broadcast domain.

📺 TV Relations

Coordination with news channels, arranging interviews, and managing television coverage of NLC events and achievements.

📻 Radio Broadcasting

Production of radio spots, interviews on All India Radio, and regional language broadcasts for community outreach.

🌐 Digital Content

Creation and distribution of online video content, webinars, virtual events, and social media video campaigns.

Key Responsibilities

Media Relations
