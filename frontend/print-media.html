Print Media

Managing press relations, publications, and print communication

About Print Media Section

The Print Media section is responsible for managing NLC India Limited's relationships with newspapers, magazines, and print publications. We handle press releases, media queries, press conferences, and ensure accurate representation of NLC in print media.

Our team works closely with journalists, editors, and publishers to disseminate information about NLC's operations, achievements, and initiatives through print channels across local, regional, and national platforms.

📰 Press Relations

Building and maintaining relationships with newspapers, magazines, and print journalists for effective media coverage.

📝 Press Releases

Drafting and distributing official press releases on important announcements, achievements, and corporate updates.

📖 Publications

Creating in-house magazines, newsletters, annual reports, and promotional literature.
