About Our Services

The Photo and Videography section of Corporate Communications Department provides comprehensive visual documentation services for all NLC India Limited activities. We maintain a professional archive of photographs and videos that capture the organization's journey, achievements, and operations.

Our team utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to create high-quality visual content for corporate use, promotional materials, annual reports, and historical documentation.

📷 Photography

Professional photography for events, operations, infrastructure, and official ceremonies with advanced DSLR and drone technology.

🎥 Videography

High-definition video production including documentaries, promotional films, training videos, and event coverage.

✂️ Post-Production

Expert editing, color correction, graphics integration, and final production for broadcast and digital platforms.

Request for Photo/Video Services

Internal departments can request photo/videography services by:

  • Submitting request through official email
  • Providing event details (date, time, venue, purpose)
  • Specifying type of coverage required
  • Advance notice of at least 3 working days
  • Coordinating with assigned photographer/videographer
