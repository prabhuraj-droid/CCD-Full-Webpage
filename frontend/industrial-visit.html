Industrial Visits

Explore NLC India's world-class facilities and witness sustainable energy production in action

About Industrial Visits

NLC India Limited welcomes educational institutions, professional organizations, and interested groups to visit our state-of-the-art facilities. Our industrial visits provide a unique opportunity to witness India's largest lignite-based power generation complex and understand the complete coal-to-power process.

Visitors gain hands-on insights into modern mining techniques, thermal power generation, renewable energy integration, and our commitment to environmental sustainability.

🏭 Mining Operations

Visit our open-cast lignite mines and observe advanced mining machinery, transportation systems, and rehabilitation efforts.

⚡ Power Generation

Tour our thermal power stations to understand the process of converting lignite into electricity with cutting-edge technology.

🌱 Environmental Initiatives

Explore our afforestation programs, water conservation projects, and sustainable development initiatives.

Who Can Visit

  • Engineering and technical students from recognized institutions
  • Research scholars pursuing studies in mining, energy, or environmental sciences
  • Professional bodies and industry associations
  • Government officials and delegations
  • Academic faculty members and researchers
  • International delegations and energy sector professionals

Visit Facilities Include

Mine I & Mine II

Experience India's largest mechanized open-cast lignite mines with advanced excavation and material handling systems.

Thermal Power Stations (TPS I & II)

Understand power generation from fuel handling to electricity transmission.

Renewable Energy Parks

Witness our solar power installations and wind energy projects.

Township & Welfare Facilities

View our model township with world-class residential and community facilities.

Application Process

  • Submit a formal request letter on institutional/organizational letterhead
  • Include proposed visit date, number of participants, and areas of interest
  • Provide details of accompanying faculty/officials
  • Submit at least 15 days in advance of proposed visit date
  • Wait for confirmation from Corporate Communications Department
  • Follow all safety guidelines and visit protocols
